      Overview

      Get through your last webcam conference of the day and then surf the net with your 15.6" Acer Aspire ES 15 laptop. It has 6GB of RAM and a 1TB hard drive so you can store and access your photos, videos, and music without a second thought. This bundle includes a black laptop carrying sleeve, 8GB USB stick, and a wireless mouse.

      More Information

      • Bundle includes a black laptop carrying sleeve, 8GB USB stick, and a wireless mouse
      • 1.8GHz AMD A4-7210 quad-core processor with 6GB of RAM delivers optimal performance for your everyday computing needs and multitasking
      • 1TB hard drive offers a compact warehouse for your movies, music, photos, and more so they're all just a click away
      • 15.6" HD CineCrystal LED-backlit LCD display with a resolution of 1366 x 768 will give you a clear viewing experience
      • Built-in 802.11b/g/n/ac Wi-Fi keeps you connected to the web from your home or anywhere with Wi-Fi
      • Integrated Bluetooth lets you wirelessly connect your Bluetooth-enabled external devices without a second thought
      • 3 USB ports, including a USB 3.0 port which delivers lightning-fast transfer speeds with USB 3.0 devices
      • HDMI connectivity means you can easily connect your computer to your TV and take movie night to the next level
      • Precision touchpad gives you accurate control as smooth as on a touchscreen for an intuitive navigation of the web, photos, and more
      • Built-in webcam delivers amazing video quality so you can enjoy a flawless video chat experience every time
      • Preinstalled with Windows 10 operating system (bilingual), which features an all-new browser, improved Start menu, digital assistant with Cortana, universal Windows apps, and much more
